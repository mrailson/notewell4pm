🎯 Getting Started

Step 1: Click the green "START RECORDING" button

Step 2: Allow microphone access when prompted

Step 3: Look for the red dot and timer (e.g., "🔴 Recording 05:23") - this confirms everything is working

Step 4: Speak normally - the AI will capture everything while the timer runs

Step 5: Click "STOP RECORDING" when finished

Step 6: Watch as AI creates your professional meeting notes!

⏱️ Understanding the Recording Timer

🔴 Timer Running = Perfect Recording: When you see the timer counting up (00:01, 00:02, etc.), your meeting is being recorded successfully.

📊 Track Meeting Length: Use the timer to monitor how long your meeting has been running and plan accordingly.

✅ Visual Confirmation: The combination of red dot + running timer means everything is working perfectly - no need to worry about technical issues.

📱 Always Visible: The timer stays visible throughout recording so you always know the system is actively capturing your discussion.

🔧 Troubleshooting & Support

Common Issues:

  • 🎤 "No microphone access": Check browser permissions in settings
  • 📢 "Poor audio quality": Move closer to speakers, reduce background noise
  • ⚡ "Processing failed": Check internet connection, try shorter recording
  • 📁 "File too large": Compress audio files or split into segments
  • 🔊 "No sound detected": Use microphone test, check system volume
  • ⏱️ "Timer not running": Restart recording - timer confirms active recording

Audio File Issues:

  • 📱 Phone recordings: Export as .mp3 or .wav for best results
  • 🎵 Large files: Use audio compression apps to reduce size
  • 🔧 Format problems: Convert to supported formats (.wav, .mp3, .m4a)
  • 📊 Quality issues: Ensure clear speech and minimal background noise

Quick Fixes:

  • Refresh page and check microphone permissions for most issues
  • Try different browsers: Chrome and Safari work best
  • Close unnecessary browser tabs for better performance
  • Use wired internet connection for large file uploads

🏥 About NoteWell

NoteWell is a comprehensive, secure meeting documentation system developed specifically for NHS primary care settings with robust information governance and patient data protection. Designed for Northamptonshire GP practices and PCNs, it transforms how practice meetings are documented while maintaining the highest standards of NHS data security.

NoteWell is currently being piloted with selected NHS practices to evaluate AI-assisted meeting documentation for practice management workflows. This initiative explores how intelligent automation can reduce administrative burden while ensuring full NHS compliance.

Your participation in this pilot helps us understand how AI tools can support NHS practice managers while maintaining gold-standard information governance.

🔒 Technical Security Architecture

🛡️ Zero-Trust Data Processing:

  • Client-Side First: All initial processing happens in your browser
  • Selective Cloud Processing: Only anonymized, sanitized content sent to AI services
  • Immediate Deletion: Audio files deleted from AI servers within seconds
  • No Persistent Storage: No databases, file servers, or long-term data retention
  • Session-Only Memory: Meeting results exist only during your browser session

🔐 Multi-Layer Encryption:

  • TLS 1.3 in Transit: All data transmission uses latest encryption standards
  • AES-256 at Rest: Any temporary data uses military-grade encryption
  • End-to-End Security: From browser to AI service with no intermediate storage
  • Certificate Pinning: Prevents man-in-the-middle attacks
